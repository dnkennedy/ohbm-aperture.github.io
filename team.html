Meet The Team.

We have four core working groups dedicated to making Aperture possible. These are each uniquely focused on community engagement, editorial direction, platform infrastructure, or workflows for publishing.

The chairs from each of the working groups — as well as liasons to the OHBM communications committee, The OHBM Publishing Initiative Committee (TOPIC), and the OHBM Council — form the Aperture steering committee.
